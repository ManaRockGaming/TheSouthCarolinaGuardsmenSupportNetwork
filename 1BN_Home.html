"Military Professionalism, Military Tradition"

The foundation of 1st Battalion rests on the motto “Military Tradition, Military Professionalism.” Every Soldier assigned to the battalion is expected to uphold this standard. Professionalism in every task, adherence to tradition in every formation, and discipline in every movement distinguish the unit as a functioning military battalion.
