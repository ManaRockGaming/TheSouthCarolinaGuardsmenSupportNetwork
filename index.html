THE SOUTH CAROLINA GUARDSMEN SUPPORT NETWORK'S MISSION:

The South Carolina Guardsmen Support Network is a non-governmental organization dedicated to strengthening the South Carolina Army National Guard, Air National Guard, and State Guard through connection, collaboration, and support. We empower service members and their families by fostering cross-branch cooperation, assisting with equipment and readiness needs, and building a unified community across all components of the South Carolina Military Department.
